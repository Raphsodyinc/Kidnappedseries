A humble Nigerian bus passenger becomes an unlikely hero when he single-handedly takes on a ruthless organ trafficking syndicate after a violent bus hijacking, exposing a deep web of police corruption and forcing him into a race against time to save innocent lives — including a woman he’s just met.

Synopsis

Bright, a hard-working Nigerian man, boards a bus to Abuja, where he meets Annabelle, a young woman from a wealthy family. The bus is ambushed by an armed human organ trafficking ring led by the ruthless Scar. With the police involved in the crime, the passengers are taken hostage.

Having narrowly escaped, Bright becomes the only hope for the captives. With time running out, he must go on a solo mission to save the lives of the hostages. His fight is a personal war against a powerful and corrupt network.
